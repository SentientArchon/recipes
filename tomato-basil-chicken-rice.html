    Nutrition Note

    Brown rice has a GI of ~55 versus ~72 for white rice, and adds 3.5 g of fiber per cup — a meaningful difference for managing post-meal glucose. The olive oil in the sauce further slows gastric emptying, flattening the glucose curve. Fresh basil is added after cooking to preserve its anti-inflammatory polyphenols. Always add a non-starchy vegetable alongside to complete the meal structure.
