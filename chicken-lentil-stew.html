    Nutrition Note

    Lentils are among the highest-fiber legumes — 16 g of fiber per cooked cup — which directly slows glucose absorption after the meal, critical for managing insulin resistance. Bone-in thighs add collagen that converts to gelatin during the long cook, supporting joint recovery from training, without any glycemic impact. Squeeze fresh lemon at serving: the vitamin C significantly increases iron absorption from the lentils.
