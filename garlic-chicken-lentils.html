    Nutrition Note

    Green lentils have a glycemic index of ~32 — one of the lowest of any carb source — releasing glucose slowly and directly blunting post-meal blood sugar spikes, which is critical for managing insulin resistance. Two cups of lentils pushes fiber to ~7 g per portion and adds substantial plant protein alongside the chicken. The orange juice is negligible per serving (about 1 1/2 tsp) — the fructose impact is minimal, and the vitamin C boosts iron absorption from the lentils. The avocado-yogurt topping adds monounsaturated fat, which slows gastric emptying and further reduces the glycemic response.
